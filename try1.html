About me

I'm Tomás. My general interests are K-Theory, Fibre Bundles & Homotopy Theory. I co-founded the Semillero Estudiantil de Investigación en Física-Matemática (SEIFM) of the Universidad Pedagógica Nacional, led by Professor Sandra Ávila. Besides, as research activities, I'm working on finding a definition of solid in terms of Graph Theory, to study graph isomorphisms via algebraic isomorphisms.
